Inventory Front End Implementation

Routing:
Inventories routing is defined in app > supplier > supplier.route.js


Translations:
All translations related to inventory module is defined in app > supplier > supplier.translate.ar.json and supplier.translate.en.json

Module:
Inventory module is all defined in app > supplier > inventories

Inventory entrance point is app > supplier > inventories > index.html

Main recipe listing is app > supplier > inventories > recipes-items-list > recipes.html

All recipes popups are defined in pp > supplier > inventories > receipt-form

Ingredients listing and ingredients add edit delete forms are defined in app > supplier > inventories > ingredient-form & app > supplier > inventories > ingredients-items-list
